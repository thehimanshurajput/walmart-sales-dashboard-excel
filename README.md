# Walmart Sales Dashboard

Interactive Excel Dashboard for Retail Sales, Customer Behavior, and Product Performance Analytics

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel • Pivot Tables • Pivot Charts • Slicers • Dashboard Design  
**Domain:** Retail Sales Analytics  
**Skills Demonstrated:** Dashboard Development • KPI Reporting • Sales Analytics • Customer Analytics • Data Visualization • Business Insights

---

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze Walmart customer purchase behavior and sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into product performance, customer demographics, repeat purchases, payment preferences, and sales trends to support data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify high-performing products and categories, understand customer purchasing patterns, and optimize marketing and inventory strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance and product demand.
- Analyze customer purchase behavior and repeat purchases.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Evaluate payment preferences and discount effectiveness.
- Analyze sales trends and geographic order distribution.

## Key Insights

- Electronics is the highest-performing product category and generates the largest number of purchases.
- Headphones are the top-selling product with 3,261 units sold.
- Customers who receive discounts exhibit slightly higher repeat purchase behavior.
- Sales remain relatively stable throughout the year, indicating consistent customer demand.
- Debit Card and Cash on Delivery are the most frequently used payment methods.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase focus on electronics and complementary products through targeted promotions and cross-selling strategies.
- Implement loyalty campaigns for customers who demonstrate repeat purchase behavior.
- Maintain adequate inventory levels for high-demand products, particularly headphones and electronics.
- Utilize customer demographics and time-based trends to create targeted marketing campaigns.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through cashback and rewards programs.
